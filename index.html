Top 3 Eco-Friendly Tips

1. Reduce Single-Use Plastics

Choose reusable bags, bottles, and containers to minimize waste.

2. Save Energy at Home

Use LED bulbs, unplug devices, and invest in energy-efficient appliances.

3. Support Local Farmers

Buying local reduces transportation emissions and supports community businesses.

Why Go Green?

  • Protects natural resources
  • Improves personal health
  • Reduces environmental footprint
